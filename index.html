Oatmeal

Ingredients 1/2 cup of quick oats 1/2 cup of water 1/2 cup of milk (optional toppings) Chocolate chips and peanut butter (optional toppings) Maple syrup, brown sugar, and cinnamon Steps Combine quick oats, water, and milk into a bowl. Microwave for 1 minute. Take out and stir the ingredients. Microwave again for 40 seconds. Take bowl out and let cool for 2 minutes. Then mix in toppings as required.
