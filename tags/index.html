Banana Bread

Ingredients 2-3 medium sized very ripe bananas 1 and 1/2 cup all purpose flour 1 pinch salt 1/2 cup sugar 1 tsp baking soda 1/3 cup butter 1 large egg 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) 1/4 cup of chocolate chips (optional) 1/4 cup of crushed nuts Steps Preheat oven to 350 F, and butter a 4x8 inch loaf pan. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until completely smooth.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients 1 (18.25 ounce) package devil’s food cake mix 1 (5.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding mix 1 cup sour cream 1 cup vegetable oil 4 eggs ½ cup warm water 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips Steps Preheat oven to 350 F. In a large bowl, mix together the cake and pudding mixes, sour cream, oil, beaten eggs and water. Stir in the chocolate chips and pour batter into a well greased 12 cup bundt pan.

Oatmeal

Ingredients 1/2 cup of quick oats 1/2 cup of water 1/2 cup of milk (optional toppings) Chocolate chips and peanut butter (optional toppings) Maple syrup, brown sugar, and cinnamon Steps Combine quick oats, water, and milk into a bowl. Microwave for 1 minute. Take out and stir the ingredients. Microwave again for 40 seconds. Take bowl out and let cool for 2 minutes. Then mix in toppings as required.

Pancakes

Ingredients 1 and 1/2 cup of quick oats 1/2 cup of flour 3 and 1/2 tsp baking soda 1 tsp salt 1 tsp cinnamon 3 tbsp butter 1 large egg 1 + 1/4 cup of milk (optional) 1 banana (optional) 1/4 cup of chocolate chips (optional) 1/4 cup of blueberries (optional toppings) Syrup, bananas, berries Steps Combine all dry ingredients in bowl (quick oats, flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and banana/berries/chocolate chips).
