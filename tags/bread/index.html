Banana Bread

Ingredients 2-3 medium sized very ripe bananas 1 and 1/2 cup all purpose flour 1 pinch salt 1/2 cup sugar 1 tsp baking soda 1/3 cup butter 1 large egg 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional) 1/4 cup of chocolate chips (optional) 1/4 cup of crushed nuts Steps Preheat oven to 350 F, and butter a 4x8 inch loaf pan. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until completely smooth.
